The Design Process

Quant developers at Tradity Capital, manage/develop low-latency trading cloud architectures, research/ideate/develop, stress-test/perform risk checks, backtest, and optimize the trading models(strategies). The trading desk quants then handle the launching, and management of the developed trading systems/portfolios. Our philosophy is that trading is all about creating a unique print, an edge that we can base our approach in developing trading systems. All trading is based on internally developed proprietary models. Quant Trading at Tradity Capital is performed according to an Algo-Development-Framework(ADF) that emphasizes on four pillars; asset selection, Alpha formulation, execution, and risk management.

Universe selection pillar

The universe selection pillar defines the assets the trading system is tasked to execute depending on the nature of the identified assets

Alpha formulation pillar

The alpha creation pillar defines the kinds of signals generated, and how they occur with regards to the trading system architecture.

Execution

The execution pillar defines how trade execution occurs, methods used in trade management

Risk management

The risk management pillar defines the mechanisms designed to properly perform risk management.
